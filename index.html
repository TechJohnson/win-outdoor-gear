Sweepstakes Rules And Regulations:

  1. Must be 18 to win.
  2. Must be a valid resident of the United States.

  4. No purchase necessary.
  5. Sweepstakes prizes are drawn annually. Sweepstakes period is April 1st through March 31st.
  6. These prizes are subject to availability. In the case that a product is not available at the time of the drawing beyond the control of the sponsor or winoutdoorgear.com, a comparable prize at the same retail value will be substituted.
  7. Prizes are non-transferable. Sweepstakes winners will be contacted by phone only and will have 90 days to claim their prize. If prize is not claimed within 90 days after prize notification, prize package will be forfeited.

  9. Our sweepstakes prize packages are provided by our sponsors. Our sponsors will call you to validate your entry and confirm your shipping address should you win a prize package. Validation by phone with our sponsors is required and during that brief phone call they will tell you about the products and services they offer. No purchase is necessary but we ask that you do listen to our sponsor’s brief message so that we can keep giving away great gear! Once you validate the entry, you can opt out of sponsor phone calls at any time by emailing us.
  10. Winoutdoorgear.com will never sell your data but will provide it to prize package sponsors for purpose of calling you to validate the entry & provide you with a brief information statement regarding their products or services.
